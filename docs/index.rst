EcosizerEngine
==============

EcosizerEngine is a Python library for sizing and simulating domestic hot
water (DHW) systems in multi-family and commercial buildings, with a focus
on heat pump water heater (HPWH) technology.

The engine performs three core functions:

* **Sizing** — calculates minimum heating capacity (kBTU/hr) and storage
  volume (gallons) required to meet peak demand.
* **3-Day Design-Day Simulation** — models system performance at 1-minute
  timesteps over three peak-demand days.
* **Annual Simulation** — full-year simulation at 10-minute timesteps for
  cost and efficiency analysis.
